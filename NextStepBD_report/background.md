# Nextstepbd — Background

## Overview
Nextstepbd is a Bangladesh-based software and automation solutions company with an experienced team of around 50 people (developers, designers, project managers). The organisation focuses on custom software development, AI-enabled automation, and IT consultancy for local and international clients across retail, healthcare, logistics and customer service.

## Mission, Vision & Objectives
- Vision: Become a leading software and automation solutions provider in South Asia by delivering intelligent, scalable systems.
- Mission: Deliver high-quality, tailored software; automate business processes through innovative technology; improve organizational efficiency via AI and integrations.
- Objectives: Improve delivery speed and quality, reduce manual coordination, centralize data, and improve client satisfaction.

## Products & Services
- Custom software systems (management systems, ERP-like platforms)
- Healthcare management systems (appointments, patient records)
- Business automation tools and AI-powered customer support
- IT consultancy and system integration services
- Social-media support automation and chat-based engagement systems

## Operational Model
- Client-driven project acquisition and scoping
- Requirement gathering via client communication channels
- Internal task allocation and milestone-driven delivery
- Post-deployment maintenance and support

## Noted Constraints and Current Challenges (summary)
- Heavy reliance on ad-hoc communication (WhatsApp) for decisions and requirements
- Multiple disconnected tools (Sheets, GitHub, chat) causing fragmentation
- Data duplication and inconsistent records across tools
- Security risks due to informal sharing of sensitive information
- Manual processes and single-person dependencies that impede scalable operations
