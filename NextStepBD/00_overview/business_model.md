# Business Model – Nextstepbd

Nextstepbd operates as a service-based software development company with consultancy capabilities.

## Revenue Streams
- Software development projects for clients
- Long-term maintenance contracts
- IT consultancy services
- Custom automation solutions

## Operational Model
- Client-based project acquisition
- Requirement gathering through client communication
- Internal task allocation to development teams
- Delivery of software products or solutions
- Post-deployment support and maintenance

## Key Characteristics
- Project-based workflow
- High dependency on client communication
- Custom-built solutions rather than standard products
